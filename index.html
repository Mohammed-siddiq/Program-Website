Application Approach With Data Structures.


Learning Data structures is one thing applying them is whole new ball game.This program introduces all the vital Data structures used and required by the industry.Insights on their applications,with complete hands on experience and live demonstrations.By the end, you will have the knack to apply and decide which Data structure to choose given an application.

Design Principles And Problem Solving Using Algorithms

What makes a programmer competent? Its, how well he/she solves a given problem. Algorithms are the backbones of any solution.What algorithmic knowledge does industry demands? What problem solving skills does industry demands? OR If you dream of building a start up,the foundation on problem solving and algorithmic analysis is a must.

Building & Consuming RESTful API.


How do web applications communicate? How is it possible to view the same content on the website and in the app. Understand the underlying and 'the predominant' style of web communciations. Be it google, facebook, twitter, or name any website, they provide information access through REST. Learn how to consume these REST API's with live demonstrations, and how to build RESTful services. By the end of the program you would have consumed and Build your own RESTful API.

Application Approach With Data Structures

This program is divide into multiple division.Find below the detailed description of each Div.

  • Div-1
    Why Study ALgorithms?


    Often times this is what we miss in the learning process,due to which the learning itself becomes hard.

    If the reason behind your actions is unknown, then it is less likely for you to succeed.Get to know WHY is it important to learn algortihms,the motivation behind it.This division puts focus on preparing your mind to learn algorithms and its implications.By Answering your "whys" first makes the learning not only easier but interesting.Following which you will be exposed to industrial applications & problem solving using algorithms.At the end of this Div, you will know why you need to study and what role does algorithms play in the industries .

  • Div -2
    Asymptotic Notations & Complexity analysis.

    Considered to be the hardest part to grasp while learning algorithms.

    Your competency on algorithms is judged based on how well you analyze given an algorithm.Asymptotic notations is "The language" used for analyzing any algorithm. Its these notations that makes learning of algorithms a daunting task. Often times the understanding of big-oh or theta notations remains a mystery, even after designing and implementing numerous algorithms. This division provides you complete understanding of all the notations and complexity classes. Giving you complete informal understanding to all the jargons. By the end of this div you will be able to analyse , and comment on the complexity of any given algorithm

  • Div-3 Searching & sorting Algorithms.

    A Common mistake is that we assume we know these algorithms.

    The real understanding of algorithms begins with searching and sorting algorithms. Knowing these algorithms is just rubbing the surface. What's important is digging deep into these algorithms , why is it they are implemented the way they are. Complete insights on the complexities and why their complexity is the way it is. Giving you live demonstrations and implementations. By the end of this div you will have complete understanding of the complexities of searching and sorting algorithms.

  • Div-4
    Divide & conquer.

    when do you approach a problem with Divide-Conquer strategy?

    Why do you choose divide and conquer strategy?How to identify a problem that can be solved using divide and conquer. These are the questions often missed.This div will answer all these.You will understand the Complexity analysis of these algorithms. Using classic problems like...., to interesting real world problems , you will gain complete understanding of the divide and conquer.Giving you complete insights on the complexity classes of these algorithms.

  • Div - 5
    Dynamic programming

    The most intuitive and interesting algorithmic strategy.

    Given any problem, you can solve it using brute force. But can you improvise?.Few problems can be so improvised that their complexities reduces drastically. Learn how to implement dynamic programming with live demonstrations and implementations.Given any problem statement, gain insights to correlate it to dynamic programming. By the end of this div you will know whether any given problem can be solved using dynamic programming and comment of their complexities.

  • Div - 6

    Fututre Algo learning
    &
    HaCkAtHoN-prep

    Learning algorithms and staying updated is a never ending process.

    But what to refer and look into, how to approach a new algorithm?, This div will provide you all the to- dos to learn algorithms in future and being on top of the game.It will also introduce a new problem statement which will be part of hackathon.You will put in all the knowledge you gained in this program to solve this problem. Technologies required to solve this problem will be shared.Preparing you for the hackathon scheduled in the next division.

  • Div - 7
    HaCkAtHoN

    Its Show Time!!!

    You will be working in teams and competing to solve the given problem.The knowledge gained from this program and a bit of creativity will be enough in cracking the problem. This is designed to boost your confidence in problem solving using algorithms. The best two teams will be rewarded(might stand a chance to intern in our start-up). Following which you will be introduced to our Advanced algorithms program.

  • Program Duration: 3 months
